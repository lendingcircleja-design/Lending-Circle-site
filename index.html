FAQs

How fast can I get approved?

Often the same day. If we have everything we need to verify your details, we move quickly. If anything is missing, we’ll let you know immediately so nothing slows you down.

What will my payments look like?

We’ll give you a simple schedule up front based on your chosen term. No jargon—just clear dates and amounts so you can plan easily.

Are there hidden fees?

No. You’ll always see the full cost before you commit. A small processing fee applies only to multi‑payment plans; we’ll flag it clearly when it’s relevant.

What if I’m salaried monthly?

Select a term that lines up with your salary date and we’ll structure things to fit. If you go beyond a single month, a one‑time processing fee may apply—we’ll let you know before you sign.
