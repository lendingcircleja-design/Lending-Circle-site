FAQs

What are the interest terms?

We keep it simple: 35% for the first fortnight, then 5% more for each additional fortnight—capped at 2 months. Before you commit, we show the full cost so there are no surprises.

How fast can I be approved?

Often the same day. If we have everything we need to verify your details, we move quickly. If anything is missing, we’ll tell you exactly what’s needed to keep things moving.

Do I have to pay a processing fee?

Only if your plan has multiple payments. If you’re paid monthly and pick 4 weeks, there’s usually no fee. Go beyond that (or beyond 2 weeks if not salaried monthly), and a one-time JMD 1,950 fee applies upfront.

Can I repay early?

Yes. If you want to settle before the end of your term, just let us know. We’ll give you the early-settlement amount—no penalty for paying off early.

Are you licensed?

Yes—we operate as a licensed microcredit institution in Jamaica. It’s important to us that you feel safe and informed at every step.
